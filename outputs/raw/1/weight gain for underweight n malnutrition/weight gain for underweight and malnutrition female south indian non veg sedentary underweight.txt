Rationale & Alternatives
This plan provides gradual healthy weight gain for sedentary underweight South
Indian females, supporting nutrient restoration and hormonal balance.
It uses animal protein (eggs, chicken, fish) for muscle repair and micronutrients (B12,
iron, zinc), combined with easy-to-digest traditional foods for comfort.

The calorie surplus (≈2400 kcal) is distributed through small frequent meals to
prevent heaviness.
