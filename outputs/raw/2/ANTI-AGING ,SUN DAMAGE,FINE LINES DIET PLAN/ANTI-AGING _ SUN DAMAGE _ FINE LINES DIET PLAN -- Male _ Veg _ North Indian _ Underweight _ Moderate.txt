Rationale & Alternatives
● Crafted for moderately active underweight males focusing on muscle and healthy fat
gain with anti-aging benefits.
● Incorporates antioxidants (Vitamin C, E, Zinc) and collagen precursors to reduce fine
lines and repair photoaged skin.
● Combines plant-based protein (dal, paneer, soy) and slow carbs (brown rice,
chapati, oats) for steady energy and recovery.
● Includes healthy fats (nuts, flaxseed, ghee) to support skin lipid barrier and prevent
dryness.
● Substitutions:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu / Soy Chunks
○ Wheat ↔ Jowar / Bajra

○ Curd ↔ Greek Yogurt
○ Flaxseed ↔ Chia / Sunflower Seeds
○ Mustard Oil ↔ Olive Oil
