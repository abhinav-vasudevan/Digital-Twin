Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for moderately active obese individuals focusing on fat loss, anti-aging,
and collagen recovery.
● Combines low-GI grains (ragi, oats, millet) for slow energy release with high plant
protein for muscle preservation.
● Includes antioxidants (vitamin C, E, zinc) and omega-3-rich foods (flaxseed, tofu) to
protect against UV damage.
● Emphasizes fiber and hydration to enhance detoxification and improve skin clarity.
● Substitutions:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu / Tempeh
○ Curd ↔ Soy Yogurt
○ Flaxseed ↔ Chia Seeds
○ Ragi ↔ Oats or Bajra

○ Olive Oil ↔ Mustard Oil
