Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for lightly active overweight individuals aiming to reduce body fat and
restore skin vitality.
● Balances low-GI carbohydrates with high plant protein to improve metabolism and
collagen formation.
● Includes anti-inflammatory and antioxidant foods (turmeric, spinach, flaxseed, tofu,
citrus) to counter UV damage.
● Encourages fiber-rich meals to regulate hormones and support detoxification.
● Substitutions:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu or Tempeh
○ Ragi ↔ Oats or Bajra
○ Curd ↔ Greek Yogurt
○ Flaxseed ↔ Pumpkin Seeds

○ Olive Oil ↔ Mustard Oil (for cooking)
