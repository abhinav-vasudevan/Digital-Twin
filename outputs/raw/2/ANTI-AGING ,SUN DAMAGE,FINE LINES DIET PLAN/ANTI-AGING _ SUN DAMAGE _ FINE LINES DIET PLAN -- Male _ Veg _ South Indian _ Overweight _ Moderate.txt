Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for moderately active overweight men to reduce body fat while preserving
muscle.
● Emphasizes low-GI carbs (millets, oats, brown rice) for steady energy.
● Promotes collagen repair, antioxidant intake, and UV protection through spinach,
turmeric, flaxseed, and papaya.
● Balances protein, omega-3, and fiber for improved metabolism and skin elasticity.
