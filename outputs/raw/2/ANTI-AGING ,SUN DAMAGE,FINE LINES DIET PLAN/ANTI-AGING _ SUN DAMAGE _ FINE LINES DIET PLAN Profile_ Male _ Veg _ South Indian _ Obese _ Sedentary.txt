Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for obese sedentary individuals focusing on fat reduction and skin
rejuvenation.
● Emphasizes high-protein, low-GI foods (ragi, oats, millet) to support metabolism and
control cravings.
● Includes antioxidant and anti-inflammatory foods (turmeric, flaxseed, spinach,
papaya) for UV protection and collagen health.
● Promotes satiety with fiber-rich meals and skin repair with omega-3s and vitamins
C, E, and zinc.
● Substitutions:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu
○ Curd ↔ Greek Yogurt
○ Ragi ↔ Oats or Bajra

○ Flaxseed ↔ Chia or Pumpkin Seeds
○ Olive Oil ↔ Mustard Oil
