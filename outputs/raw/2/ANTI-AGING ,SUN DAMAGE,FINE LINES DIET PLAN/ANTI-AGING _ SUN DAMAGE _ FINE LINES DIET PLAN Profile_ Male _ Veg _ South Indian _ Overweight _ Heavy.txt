Rationale & Alternatives
● Suitable for heavy activity to improve endurance and skin elasticity without excess
calorie surplus.
● Uses slow-digesting carbs (ragi, millets, brown rice) to maintain energy while
promoting fat oxidation.
● Emphasizes antioxidants and collagen precursors — vitamins C, E, and zinc — to
reduce wrinkles and pigmentation.
● High-protein structure supports muscle recovery and collagen synthesis.
