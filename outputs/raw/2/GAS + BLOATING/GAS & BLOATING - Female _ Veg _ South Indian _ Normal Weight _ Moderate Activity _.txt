Dietary & Cultural Context
• Vegetarian South Indian diet
• Mild spice, minimal oil
• Avoids heavy dals (chana, rajma), cruciferous vegetables (cauliflower, cabbage raw), fried
items
• Uses digestion-friendly foods: moong dal, rice, curd, ginger, jeera
Rationale & Alternatives
• Warm liquids ease gas and bloating
• Light meals prevent heaviness
• Moong dal is the easiest lentil to digest
• Low-FODMAP foods soothe the gut
Alternatives:
• Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
• Rice ↔ Foxtail millet
• Curd ↔ Probiotic buttermilk
• Idli ↔ Appam
