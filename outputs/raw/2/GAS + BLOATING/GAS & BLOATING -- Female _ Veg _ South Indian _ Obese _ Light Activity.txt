Dietary & Cultural Context
• Vegetarian, South Indian
• Mild spice, minimal oil
• Avoid gas-producing foods: chana, chole, cauliflower, raw cabbage, fried foods
• Preferred oils: sesame oil, coconut oil
Rationale & Alternatives
• Low-FODMAP foods to reduce gas and bloating
• Controlled calorie intake for weight management
• Warm liquids to ease digestion
• Avoid fermentation and heavy dals
Alternatives:
• Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
• Rice ↔ Foxtail millet
• Curd ↔ Probiotic buttermilk
