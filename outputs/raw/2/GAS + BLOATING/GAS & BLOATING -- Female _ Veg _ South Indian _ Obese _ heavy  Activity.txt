● Method: Boil cumin in water, strain, drink warm, almonds raw
● Serving Size: 1 glass + almonds
● Prep Time: 5 min
● Nutritive Values: 40 kcal | 1 g protein | 3 g carbs | 2 g fat | 0.5 g fiber

Breakfast (8:00–8:30 AM)
Option 1 – Ragi Idli + Vegetable Sambar (low spice)
● Ingredients: Ragi flour 1 cup, mixed vegetables ½ cup, toor dal ¼ cup, mild spices
● Method: Prepare idli batter, steam idlis, cook sambar with vegetables and dal, serve
warm
● Serving Size: 4 idlis + 1 small bowl sambar
● Prep Time: 20 min
● Nutritive Values: 350 kcal | 12 g protein | 60 g carbs | 8 g fat | 4 g fiber
Option 2 – Moong Dal Cheela + Mint Chutney
● Ingredients: Moong dal ½ cup, ginger ¼ tsp, cumin ¼ tsp, mint 5 leaves, lemon ½ tsp
● Method: Soak and grind dal with spices, pan roast into cheelas, serve with chutney
● Serving Size: 2 cheelas + 1 tsp chutney
● Prep Time: 20 min
● Nutritive Values: 320 kcal | 18 g protein | 42 g carbs | 5 g fat | 4 g fiber
Option 3 – Vegetable Upma + Buttermilk
● Ingredients: Semolina 1 cup, carrot 2 tbsp, peas 1 tbsp, low-fat buttermilk 200 ml
● Method: Lightly sauté vegetables, cook semolina in water, serve with buttermilk
● Serving Size: 1 bowl upma + 1 glass buttermilk
● Prep Time: 20 min
● Nutritive Values: 340 kcal | 10 g protein | 55 g carbs | 7 g fat | 4 g fiber
