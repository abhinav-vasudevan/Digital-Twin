Dietary & Cultural Context
● Allergens: Dairy, Nuts, Gluten
● Diet Type: Vegetarian
● Cultural Preference: North Indian
● Sodium Restriction: < 1500 mg/day
Rationale & Alternatives
● Focuses on easily digestible proteins (moong dal, paneer, tofu)
● Avoids gas-forming legumes like rajma, chickpeas, cabbage, onion, and garlic in large
quantities
● Uses herbs and spices like mint, cumin, fennel, coriander, ginger to reduce bloating
● Encourages hydration with herbal teas, warm water, and buttermilk
● Promotes gut-friendly fibers that aid digestion without causing gas
Substitutions:
● Paneer ↔ Tofu
● Ragi ↔ Oats / Millet

● Curd ↔ Soy Yogurt
● Onion/Garlic ↔ Asafoetida (Hing) or herbs
