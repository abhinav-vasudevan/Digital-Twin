Rationale & Alternatives
• Low-FODMAP foods reduce gas formation
• Light dal (moong) prevents bloating
• Warm liquids improve gut motility
• Avoid cabbage, cauliflower, chana, chole, rajma, fried foods
Alternatives:
• Moong Dal ↔ Masoor Dal (washed twice)
• Rice ↔ Millet (little millet, foxtail)
• Buttermilk ↔ Coconut water
• Idli ↔ Appam
