Early Morning (6:30–7:00 AM)

Dish: Warm Water + Lemon + Fennel Seeds + Ginger
● Ingredients: Warm water 200 ml, lemon juice 1 tsp, fennel seeds ½ tsp, grated ginger ¼
tsp
● Method: Boil fennel seeds and ginger in water for 3–5 min, strain, add lemon juice, drink
warm
● Time: 5 min
● Serving Size: 1 glass
● Nutritive Values: 20 kcal | 0.5 g protein | 3 g carbs | 0 g fat | 1 g fiber
● Tip: Promotes digestion, reduces bloating, and stimulates metabolism. Avoid cold drinks
in the morning.

● Serving Size: 2 chillas + 2 tbsp chutney + 1 cup tea

● Tip: High-protein breakfast reduces hunger and supports digestion.

Dietary & Cultural Context
● Allergens: Dairy, Nuts, Gluten
● Diet Type: Vegetarian
● Cultural Preference: North Indian
● Sodium Restriction: < 2000 mg/day
● Focus on low-gas legumes, steamed vegetables, and fermented foods.
Rationale & Alternatives
● Developed for overweight women with moderate activity and moderate gas/bloating.
● High in plant proteins (dal, paneer, soy) to support satiety.
● Fiber-rich foods (oats, vegetables, fruits) improve gut motility and reduce bloating.
● Fermented foods (curd, buttermilk) promote healthy gut microbiota.
● Avoid fried, processed, and carbonated foods that increase gas.
● Substitutions:

○ Paneer ↔ Tofu
○ Brown rice ↔ Quinoa / Millet
○ Roti ↔ Millet or Bajra Roti
○ Herbal tea ↔ Green tea
