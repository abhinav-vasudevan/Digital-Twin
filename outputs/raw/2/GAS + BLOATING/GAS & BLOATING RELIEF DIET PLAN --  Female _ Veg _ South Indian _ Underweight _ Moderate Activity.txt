RATIONALE & ALTERNATIVES
• Balanced plan for digestion + healthy weight gain
• Low-FODMAP based foods decrease gas and bloating
• Soft-texture foods reduce abdominal discomfort

● Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal (strained)
● Poha ↔ Upma
● Idli ↔ Dosa (soft)
● Banana ↔ Papaya
