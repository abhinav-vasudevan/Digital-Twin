RATIONALE & ALTERNATIVES
• Heavy activity requires a calorie surplus
• Diet supports digestion, reduces gas, and boosts weight gain
• Soft, easy-to-digest South Indian foods reduce bloating
• Alternatives:
● Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
● Upma ↔ Poha
● Idli ↔ Dosa (soft)
● Banana ↔ Sweet potato
