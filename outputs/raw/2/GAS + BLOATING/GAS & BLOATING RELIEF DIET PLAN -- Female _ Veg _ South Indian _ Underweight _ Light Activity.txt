RATIONALE & ALTERNATIVES
• Uses low-FODMAP South Indian foods to reduce bloating
• Soft-texture meals reduce stomach irritation
• Includes calorie-dense but gentle foods for healthy weight gain
• Alternatives:
● Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal (strained)
● Idli ↔ Dosa (soft)
● Papaya ↔ Banana
● Ajwain water ↔ Jeera water
