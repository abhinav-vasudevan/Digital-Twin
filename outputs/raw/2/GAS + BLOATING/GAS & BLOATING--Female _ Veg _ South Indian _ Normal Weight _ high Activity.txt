Dietary & Cultural Context
● Vegetarian, South Indian
● Mild spice, minimal oil
● Avoid: chole, rajma, cauliflower, broccoli, raw onion, cabbage raw, fried items
● Preferred oils: sesame, coconut
Rationale & Alternatives
● Higher calories for heavy activity
● High protein but light-digestion foods
● Warm liquids reduce gas
● No fermented, no heavy dals

Alternatives:
● Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
● Rice ↔ Little millet
● Curd ↔ Buttermilk
● Bottle gourd ↔ Ridge gourd / Ash gourd
