Rationale & Alternatives
● Heavy activity requires higher protein and calories; meals include digestible proteins,
light curries, and fiber-rich foods to reduce bloating.
● Inclusion of ginger, fennel, and fermented foods supports gut health.
● Substitutions:
○ Fish ↔ Eggs / Tofu
○ Ragi flour ↔ Oats / Millets
○ Milk ↔ Soy / Almond milk
○ Vegetables ↔ Seasonal fibrous vegetables
