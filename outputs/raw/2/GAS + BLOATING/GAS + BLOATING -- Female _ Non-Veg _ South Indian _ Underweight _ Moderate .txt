Rationale & Alternatives
● Moderate meals with easily digestible proteins for underweight, moderately active
females.
● Inclusion of ginger, fennel, light curries, and fermented foods to reduce gas.
● Substitutions:
○ Fish ↔ Eggs / Tofu
○ Ragi flour ↔ Oats / Millets
○ Milk ↔ Soy / Almond milk

○ Vegetables ↔ Seasonal fibrous vegetables
