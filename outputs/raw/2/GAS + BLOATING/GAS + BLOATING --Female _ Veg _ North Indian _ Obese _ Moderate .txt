Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for obese individuals with moderate gas and bloating.
● Promotes digestion with fennel, ginger, mint, and lightly sautéed or steamed vegetables.
● High fiber and moderate protein to maintain satiety without bloating.
● Avoids heavy, oily, fried, and highly processed foods.
● Substitutions:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu
○ Wheat chapati ↔ Ragi or Millet roti
○ Low-fat yogurt ↔ Soy yogurt
○ Green vegetables ↔ Seasonal fibrous veggies
