Dietary & Cultural Context
● Focus on North Indian vegetarian foods.
● Avoid gas-forming foods like cabbage, cauliflower, rajma, chana in large quantities.
● Favor soaked and sprouted legumes, steamed vegetables, and gut-friendly spices
(ginger, fennel, mint, asafoetida).
● Moderate sodium, avoid fried snacks and carbonated drinks.
Rationale & Alternatives

● Rationale:
○ High-fiber, low-fat foods to reduce bloating and manage obesity.
○ Easily digestible legumes and vegetables.
○ Use of carminative spices (fennel, ginger, mint, asafoetida) to reduce gas
formation.
○ Smaller frequent meals reduce bloating and improve satiety.
● Alternatives:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu
○ Wheat Chapati ↔ Millet Roti / Oats Roti
○ Milk ↔ Soy / Almond Milk
○ High-FODMAP veggies ↔ Carrot, Bottle Gourd, Pumpkin
