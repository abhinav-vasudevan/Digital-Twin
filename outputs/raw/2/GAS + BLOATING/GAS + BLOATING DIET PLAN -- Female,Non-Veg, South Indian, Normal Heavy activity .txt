Dietary & Cultural Context
● Allergens: Dairy, Nuts, Gluten, Fish, Eggs
● Diet Type: Non-Vegetarian
● Cultural Preference: South Indian
● Sodium Restriction: < 2000 mg/day
Rationale & Alternatives
● Focused on reducing gas and bloating with easily digestible meals.
● High protein from fish, chicken, and eggs supports muscle recovery for heavy activity.
● Fiber from low-FODMAP vegetables and millet reduces gut discomfort.
● Avoids fried, oily, and heavy curries.
● Substitutions:
○ Fish ↔ Chicken
○ Ragi / Foxtail Millet ↔ Quinoa
○ Yogurt ↔ Soy Yogurt
○ Mint Chutney ↔ Coriander Chutney (no salt)
