Early Morning (6:30–7:00 AM)
Dish: Warm Water + Lemon + Fennel Seeds + Ginger
● Ingredients: Warm water 200 ml, lemon juice 1 tsp, fennel seeds ½ tsp, grated ginger ¼
tsp
● Method: Boil fennel seeds and ginger in water for 3–5 min, strain, add lemon juice, drink
warm
● Time: 5 min
● Serving Size: 1 glass
● Nutritive Values: 20 kcal | 0.5 g protein | 3 g carbs | 0 g fat | 1 g fiber
● Tip: Promotes digestion and reduces bloating. Avoid cold drinks in the morning.

Dietary & Cultural Context
● Allergens: Dairy, Nuts, Fish
● Diet Type: Non-Vegetarian
● Cultural Preference: South Indian
● Sodium Restriction: < 2000 mg/day
Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for normal-weight sedentary females experiencing moderate gas and
bloating.
● Promotes easily digestible, low-fat, low-sodium, fiber-rich foods.
● Probiotic foods (curd, buttermilk) aid gut health.
● Avoid fried, packaged, or highly processed foods.
● Fennel, ginger, lemon, mint help reduce bloating.
● Protein from eggs, fish, dal ensures satiety without heaviness.
● Substitutions:
○ Fish ↔ Egg / Tofu

○ Chapati ↔ Millet Roti / Rice
○ Coconut Chutney ↔ Mint / Coriander Chutney (low salt)
