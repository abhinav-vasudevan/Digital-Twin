Early Morning (6:30–7:00 AM)

● Ingredients: Warm water 1 glass (200 ml), lemon juice 1 tsp, fennel seeds ½ tsp, grated
ginger ¼ tsp
● Method: Boil fennel seeds and ginger in water for 3–5 min, strain, add lemon juice, drink
warm
● Time: 5 min
● Serving Size: 1 glass
● Nutritive Values: 20 kcal | 0.5 g protein | 3 g carbs | 0 g fat | 1 g fiber
● Tip: Promotes digestion, reduces bloating; avoid cold drinks in the morning

Rationale & Alternatives
● Focused on reducing gas and bloating with light, easily digestible meals.
● Promotes fiber from low-FODMAP vegetables, fruits, and millet.
● Lean protein sources (fish, eggs, chicken) support satiety and prevent gut discomfort.
● Avoids fried, heavy curries, and processed foods.
● Substitutions:
○ Fish ↔ Chicken (light preparation)
○ Ragi / Foxtail Millet ↔ Quinoa
○ Yogurt ↔ Soy Yogurt
○ Mint Chutney ↔ Coriander Chutney (no added salt)
