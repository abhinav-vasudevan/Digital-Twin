Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for obese individuals with heavy gas and bloating.
● Ajwain, fennel, ginger, cumin reduce gas formation.
● Fermented foods (idli, dhokla) improve gut flora.
● Avoid beans, fried, oily, spicy food at lunch/dinner to reduce bloating.
● Substitutions:
○ Paneer ↔ Tofu
○ Wheat chapati ↔ Ragi or Millet roti
○ Low-fat yogurt ↔ Soy yogurt

○ Vegetables ↔ Seasonal fibrous vegetables
