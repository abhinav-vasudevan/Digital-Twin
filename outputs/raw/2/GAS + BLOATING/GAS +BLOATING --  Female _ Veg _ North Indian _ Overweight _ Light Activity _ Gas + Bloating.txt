Dietary & Cultural Context
● Vegetarian, North Indian staples, mild spices, low-FODMAP
● Focus on warm, cooked foods to relieve bloating
● Avoid raw cruciferous vegetables, beans, fried foods
● Emphasis on small meals, hydration, probiotics
Rationale & Alternatives
● Protein: Moong dal, paneer, soy milk → satiety & muscle maintenance
● Carbs: Rice, ragi, quinoa → low glycemic, gentle on digestion
● Digestive spices: Fennel, ginger, mint → reduce gas
● Alternatives: Paneer ↔ Tofu, Ragi ↔ Oats/Bajra, Mint ↔ Coriander, Curd ↔ Soy yogurt
Tips for Gas & Bloating
1. Eat small, frequent meals
2. Chew slowly; avoid gulping air
3. Drink warm water or herbal teas
4. Include probiotic/fermented foods
5. Gentle post-meal walk (10–15 min)
6. Avoid raw beans, cabbage, cauliflower, carbonated drinks
