Dietary & Cultural Context
● Vegetarian, North Indian, mild spice, minimal oil
● Avoid raw cruciferous vegetables, fried foods, and overly spicy dishes
● Preferred oils: Ghee, sesame oil, minimal mustard oil
Rationale & Alternatives
● Early Morning: Hydrates, supports digestion, provides healthy fats (alternatives: fennel
tea, turmeric milk)
● Breakfast: High-quality protein & complex carbs, easy digestion (alternatives: upma,
moong chilla, oats porridge)
● Mid-Morning Snack: Adds calories & micronutrients (alternatives: banana, papaya,
soaked nuts)
● Lunch: Balanced macro-nutrients for energy & weight gain (alternatives: khichdi, chapati
+ vegetables)
● Evening Snack: Protein and energy boost (alternatives: roasted makhana, sprouts,
dates with tea)
● Dinner: Light & digestible to avoid night discomfort (alternatives: vegetable khichdi,
chapati + curry)
● Bedtime: Warm liquids for digestion and slow calories (alternatives: turmeric milk,
ajwain water, chamomile tea)
