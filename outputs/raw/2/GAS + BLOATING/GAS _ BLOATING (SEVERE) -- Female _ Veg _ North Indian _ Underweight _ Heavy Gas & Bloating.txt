Dietary & Cultural Context
● Allergens: Dairy, gluten, nuts (alternatives given)
● Avoid high-FODMAP legumes (rajma, chole, black chana, urad dal)
● Avoid heavy masala, onion, garlic
● Uses North Indian cooking patterns
● High-calorie additions kept gentle on gut
Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed for severe gas & bloating with underweight
● Uses lowest gas-forming vegetables: lauki, tinda, ghiya, carrots, beans
● Uses moong dal as primary protein (best tolerated)
● Limits fiber to prevent fermentation
● Encourages soft, warm meals
● Includes calorie boosters (ghee, potatoes, curd, dal) without triggering gas
Substitutions:
● Wheat roti ↔ Rice roti
● Curd ↔ Coconut yogurt
● Paneer ↔ Tofu
● Ajwain water ↔ Ginger–saunf water
● Rice ↔ Daliya (if tolerated)
