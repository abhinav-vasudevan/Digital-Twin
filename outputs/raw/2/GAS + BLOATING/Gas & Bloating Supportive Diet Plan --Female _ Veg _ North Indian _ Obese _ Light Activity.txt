Early Morning (6:30–7:00 AM)
Dish: Warm Water + Lemon + Fennel Seeds + Ginger
● Ingredients: Warm water 200 ml, lemon juice 1 tsp, fennel seeds ½ tsp, grated ginger ¼
tsp
● Method: Boil fennel seeds and ginger in water for 3–5 min, strain, add lemon juice, drink
warm
● Time: 5 min
● Serving Size: 1 glass
● Nutritive Values: 20 kcal | 0.5 g protein | 3 g carbs | 0 g fat | 1 g fiber
● Tip: Promotes digestion, relieves bloating and gas. Avoid cold drinks in the morning.
Breakfast (8:00–8:30 AM)
Option 1 – Moong Dal Chilla + Mint Chutney + Green Tea

● Method: Soak dal 2–3 h, grind with ginger and mint, pan-fry lightly
● Time: 20 min
● Serving Size: 2 chillas + 2 tbsp chutney + 1 cup tea
● Nutritive Values: 350 kcal | 18 g protein | 40 g carbs | 8 g fat | 5 g fiber
● Tip: Use minimal oil; mint helps reduce gas.

Option 2 – Vegetable Upma (Rava / Semolina) + Buttermilk (½ glass)

● Method: Roast rava lightly, cook with vegetables, temper with cumin
● Time: 15 min
● Serving Size: 1 bowl + ½ glass buttermilk
● Nutritive Values: 360 kcal | 12 g protein | 45 g carbs | 7 g fat | 6 g fiber
● Tip: Soak rava for 10 min before cooking to reduce bloating.
Option 3 – Oats Porridge with Fennel + Cinnamon

Rationale & Alternatives
● High in fennel, mint, cumin, ginger, cinnamon – all reduce gas and bloating
● Easily digestible grains (quinoa, ragi, millet, oats) to prevent constipation
● Light, small meals with fermented foods (curd, buttermilk) improve gut microbiome
● Avoids refined, fried, spicy foods that trigger bloating
● Substitutions:
○ Brown rice ↔ Quinoa / Foxtail millet
○ Curd ↔ Soy yogurt
○ Mint chutney ↔ Coriander chutney
○ Makhana ↔ Roasted pumpkin seeds
