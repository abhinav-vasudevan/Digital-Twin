Dietary & Cultural Context
● Vegetarian, North Indian staples, low-FODMAP, lightly spiced.
● Emphasizes warm, cooked foods (ginger, fennel, mint) to ease digestion.
● Avoids raw cruciferous vegetables, beans, high-fat fried foods.
● Supports high activity energy needs with protein-rich meals.
Rationale & Alternatives
● Protein: Moong dal, paneer, tofu, soy milk → muscle repair and satiety
● Carbs: Rice, quinoa, ragi, idli → sustained energy for high activity
● Digestive spices: Ginger, fennel, mint → reduce bloating/gas
● Avoid: Beans, cabbage, cauliflower, onion → reduce gas
● Alternatives: Paneer ↔ Tofu, Ragi ↔ Oats/Bajra, Mint ↔ Coriander, Curd ↔ Soy Yogurt
Tips for Gas & Bloating (High Activity)
1. Eat small, frequent meals; avoid overeating pre/post workouts
2. Chew slowly; avoid gulping air
3. Hydrate with warm water or herbal teas
4. Include probiotic/fermented foods for gut health
5. Limit raw beans, cruciferous veggies, carbonated drinks

6. Light post-meal walks or gentle stretches improve digestion
