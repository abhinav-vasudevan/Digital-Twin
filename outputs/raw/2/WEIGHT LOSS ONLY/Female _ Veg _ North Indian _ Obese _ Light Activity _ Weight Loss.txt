Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat slowly and chew well.
2. Replace sweets and bakery items with fruits.
3. Walk 10 minutes after each meal.
4. Limit portion sizes.
5. Avoid skipping meals.
6. Stay hydrated throughout the day.
7. Include strength-based activities twice weekly.
8. Avoid eating late at night.
9. Prefer steamed or grilled foods.
10. Maintain a sleep schedule (7–8 hrs).
Rationale & Alternatives
This diet ensures a calorie deficit without compromising nutrition. High protein and fiber aid
satiety and fat loss.
