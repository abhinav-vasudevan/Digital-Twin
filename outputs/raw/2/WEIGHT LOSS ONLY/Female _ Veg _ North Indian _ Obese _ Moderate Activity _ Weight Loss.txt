Dietary & Cultural Context

Allergens: Dairy, gluten, nuts (adjust per tolerance)
Tips for Weight Loss
1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals to reduce overeating.
2. Avoid late-night snacking and fried foods.
3. Use smaller plates for portion control.
4. Walk for 15 minutes after each meal.
5. Replace sweets with fruits like papaya or guava.
6. Ensure 7–8 hours of sleep daily.
7. Avoid fruit juices; prefer whole fruits.
8. Cook with minimal oil.
9. Practice deep breathing or yoga.
10. Maintain consistency for at least 8–12 weeks.
Rationale & Alternatives
This diet creates a moderate calorie deficit while maintaining muscle mass and energy for
activity. Balanced with protein, fiber, and complex carbs for satiety and steady fat burn.
