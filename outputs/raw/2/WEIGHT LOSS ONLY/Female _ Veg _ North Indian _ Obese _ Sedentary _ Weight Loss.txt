Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat smaller, frequent meals every 3 hours.
2. Avoid sugar, white flour, and fried foods.
3. Limit salt and avoid packaged snacks.
4. Drink 3 liters of water daily.
5. Include short walks after meals.
6. Eat dinner early (before 8 PM).
7. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
8. Prefer home-cooked meals over takeout.
9. Avoid fruit juices—eat whole fruits.
10. Practice deep breathing or yoga for metabolism.
Rationale & Alternatives
This diet is low-calorie, high-fiber, and protein-balanced to promote fat loss while maintaining
muscle tone. The foods are anti-inflammatory and easy to digest for sedentary individuals.
