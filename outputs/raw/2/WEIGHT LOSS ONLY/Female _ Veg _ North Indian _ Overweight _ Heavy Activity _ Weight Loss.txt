Weight Loss Tips
1. Eat protein with every meal for muscle protection.
2. Avoid fried, sugary, and processed foods.
3. Hydrate consistently throughout the day.
4. Keep meal gaps under 3 hours.
5. Include 45–60 min of exercise daily.
6. Take dinner 2–3 hours before bedtime.
7. Avoid eating while watching screens.
8. Get 7–8 hours of quality sleep.

9. Focus on strength training 3–4 days/week.
10. Track weight weekly (same time, same conditions).
Rationale & Alternatives
This plan supports calorie deficit while meeting higher protein needs for physically active
women. Balanced macros ensure lean muscle retention and efficient fat metabolism.
