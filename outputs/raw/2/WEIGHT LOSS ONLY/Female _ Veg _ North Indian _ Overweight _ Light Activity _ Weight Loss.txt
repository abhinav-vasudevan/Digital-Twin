Rationale & Alternatives
This plan supports gradual fat loss, improved digestion, and balanced nutrition for sedentary or
lightly active overweight women.
Protein and fiber are prioritized to promote satiety and metabolic stability, while carbohydrates
are moderate and sourced from whole grains.
Substitutions:
● Paneer ↔ Tofu
● Brown rice ↔ Millet or quinoa
● Buttermilk ↔ Coconut water
● Poha ↔ Oats upma
Tips for Weight Loss
1. Keep daily calorie intake 500 kcal below maintenance level.
2. Avoid sugar, bakery items, and deep-fried foods.
3. Walk or stretch for 15–20 minutes after meals.

4. Eat slowly; stop when 80% full.
5. Drink 2.5–3 L water per day.
6. Get at least 7 hours of sleep.
7. Practice mindful eating—no screens during meals.
8. Replace evening tea snacks with roasted or steamed options.
9. Prefer home-cooked meals over packaged food.
10. Track weight and waist circumference weekly.
