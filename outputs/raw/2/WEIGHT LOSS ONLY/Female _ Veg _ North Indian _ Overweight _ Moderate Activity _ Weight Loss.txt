Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat 4–5 small, balanced meals daily.
2. Avoid sugar, processed foods, and refined flour.
3. Stay hydrated (3 L/day).
4. Walk or stretch after every meal.
5. Eat dinner at least 2 hours before bed.
6. Limit salt and caffeine.
7. Practice portion control.
8. Include protein with every meal.
9. Prefer steamed, grilled, or sautéed foods.
10. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
Rationale & Alternatives
This plan promotes gradual and sustainable weight loss with sufficient protein and fiber for
satiety and muscle support. It’s tailored for moderately active females and ensures energy
balance without deprivation.
