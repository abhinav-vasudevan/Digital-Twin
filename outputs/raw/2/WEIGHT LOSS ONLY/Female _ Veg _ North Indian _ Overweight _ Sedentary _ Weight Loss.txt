Rationale & Alternatives
Designed for overweight women with sedentary lifestyle targeting fat loss through low-calorie,

Focuses on digestive health, stable blood sugar, and nutrient adequacy with limited oil and salt.

Tips for Weight Loss
1. Stay in a calorie deficit (500 kcal below maintenance).
2. Eat every 3–4 hours to maintain metabolism.
3. Avoid sugar, processed snacks, and fried foods.
4. Prefer steaming or sautéing instead of frying.
5. Limit oil to 2 tsp/day.
6. Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily.
7. Sleep 7–8 hours to regulate hormones.
8. Add 30 min daily walk or light yoga.

9. Avoid eating late at night.
10. Keep a weekly progress journal.
