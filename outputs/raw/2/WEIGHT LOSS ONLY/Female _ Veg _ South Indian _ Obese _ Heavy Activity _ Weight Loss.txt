Rationale & Alternatives
Formulated for obese women with heavy activity to aid fat oxidation, maintain lean mass, and
prevent fatigue.
Balanced macronutrient distribution supports stamina and post-exercise recovery.
High-protein, moderate-fat, and low-glycemic carbohydrate foods prevent insulin spikes and
inflammation.

Tips for Weight Loss
1. Maintain a calorie deficit but ensure adequate protein for muscle recovery.
2. Avoid fried, sugary, and refined foods.
3. Keep post-workout meals protein-rich.

4. Hydrate with at least 3 liters of water daily.
5. Limit salt and oil (max 2 tsp/day).
6. Include probiotics daily (curd/buttermilk).
7. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
8. Do not skip breakfast.
9. Prefer steaming and grilling over frying.
10. Track weight and inch loss weekly.
