Rationale & Alternatives
Designed for obese females with moderate activity to promote gradual fat loss, improve
metabolic efficiency, and preserve lean muscle.
Uses complex carbs, high-protein meals, and anti-inflammatory ingredients for sustained
energy and satiety.
Limits oil and sodium to control body fat percentage and reduce water retention.

Tips for Weight Loss
1. Follow a calorie deficit of 400–500 kcal/day.
2. Maintain regular meal timing every 3 hours.
3. Avoid refined foods and excess salt.

4. Limit oil to 2 tsp/day and prefer steaming or sautéing.
5. Include daily 45 minutes of physical activity.
6. Hydrate well with 2.5–3 liters of water/day.
7. Avoid eating post 9:00 PM.
8. Include protein in every meal.
9. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
10. Track progress weekly (inches, energy levels, mood).
