Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat small, frequent, balanced meals every 3 hours.
2. Avoid fried, processed, or sugary foods.
3. Limit oil to 2 tsp/day and prefer cold-pressed oils.
4. Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily.

5. Add 20–30 minutes of daily walking or yoga.
6. Increase fiber intake through vegetables and millets.
7. Maintain consistent meal timing.
8. Avoid eating after 9:00 PM.
9. Sleep 7–8 hours per night for better hormonal balance.
10. Practice mindful eating—eat slowly and stop when 80% full.
