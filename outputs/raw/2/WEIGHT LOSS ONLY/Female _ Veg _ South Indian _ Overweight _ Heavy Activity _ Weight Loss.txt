Tips for Weight Loss
1. Maintain a calorie deficit of 400–500 kcal/day.
2. Always include protein post-workout.
3. Avoid late-night snacking or sugary drinks.
4. Include probiotics daily (curd or yogurt).
5. Use cold-pressed oils sparingly (1–2 tsp/day).
6. Focus on hydration (3 liters/day minimum).
7. Prioritize sleep and muscle recovery.
8. Combine strength and cardio training.
9. Limit fruit intake to 1–2 portions/day.
10. Use spices like turmeric and ginger for anti-inflammatory benefits.
