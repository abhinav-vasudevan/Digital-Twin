Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat 4–5 small meals spaced 3–4 hours apart.
2. Prioritize protein-rich foods like dal, paneer, tofu, sprouts.
3. Avoid sugar, refined flour, and packaged foods.
4. Stay hydrated with 2.5–3 liters of water daily.

5. Include fiber-rich vegetables in every meal.
6. Practice portion control and mindful eating.
7. Limit salt and avoid late-night snacking.
8. Engage in daily exercise for 45 minutes.
9. Sleep 7–8 hours each night.
10. Track progress through waist measurement and stamina.
