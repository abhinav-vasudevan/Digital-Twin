Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat 4–5 small, balanced meals daily.
2. Include one protein source in every meal.
3. Avoid refined flour, sugar, and fried items.
4. Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily.
5. Use cold-pressed oils sparingly.
6. Replace milk tea with green or herbal teas.
7. Walk or stretch for 30 minutes every day.
8. Avoid skipping meals or eating late at night.
9. Get 7–8 hours of quality sleep daily.
10. Track progress through energy and inch loss, not just body weight.
