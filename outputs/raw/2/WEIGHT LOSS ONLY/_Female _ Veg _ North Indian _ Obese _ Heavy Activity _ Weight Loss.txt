Dietary & Cultural Context
Diet Type: Vegetarian
Cuisine: North Indian
Cooking Oil: Mustard or Olive Oil (max 2 tsp/day)
Flavors: Mild spice, low salt, minimal oil
Avoid: Deep-fried, refined flour, sweets, packaged snacks
Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat small frequent meals every 3 hours.
2. Avoid sugar and processed foods.
3. Drink 3–3.5 L water daily.
4. Avoid eating after 8 PM.

5. Prefer boiling, steaming, and grilling methods.
6. Walk for 10 minutes after meals.
7. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
8. Limit salt and oily foods.
9. Replace milk tea with green or herbal teas.
10. Track weight weekly and note changes.
Rationale & Alternatives
This plan is designed to maintain a moderate calorie deficit while supporting heavy activity
levels. It provides balanced macronutrients to promote fat loss, maintain muscle tone, and
improve metabolism.
Alternatives: Paneer ↔ Tofu, Rajma ↔ Chickpeas, Brown Rice ↔ Millets, Buttermilk ↔
Coconut Water
