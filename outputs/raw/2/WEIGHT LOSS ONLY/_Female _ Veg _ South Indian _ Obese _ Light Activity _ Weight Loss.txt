Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat smaller, frequent meals every 3 hours.
2. Avoid fried, sugary, and refined foods.
3. Limit oil to 2 tsp/day.
4. Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily.
5. Add daily walking (30–40 mins).
6. Include more green vegetables and salads.
7. Avoid late-night meals.
8. Sleep for 7–8 hours daily.

9. Practice portion control.
10. Choose millets over white rice regularly.
