Tips for Weight Loss
1. Eat 4–5 small meals every 3–4 hours.
2. Include protein in every meal.
3. Avoid sugar, refined flour, and deep-fried foods.
4. Stay hydrated with 2.5–3 liters of water daily.
5. Use cold-pressed oils in moderation.
6. Replace milk tea with green or herbal tea.
7. Walk for at least 30–40 minutes daily.
8. Avoid eating after 9:00 PM.
9. Sleep for 7–8 hours daily.
10. Monitor progress through energy levels and inch loss.
