Rationale & Alternatives
● Designed to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress while improving nutrient
absorption.
● Emphasizes omega-3 fats, plant proteins, and antioxidants (flaxseed, turmeric,
spinach, coconut, papaya).
● Avoids refined sugar, hydrogenated oils, excessive dairy, and deep-fried foods.
● Supports healthy weight gain through complex carbs + moderate fats.
