Tips for Weight Gain + PCOS

1. Eat 5–6 nutrient-dense meals/day.
2. Include protein + healthy fats in every meal.
3. Moderate GI carbs for steady energy.
4. Moderate exercise like walking, yoga, or light strength training 3–4 times/week.
5. Avoid fried & ultra-processed foods.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours/day.
7. Be consistent for at least 12 weeks.
Rationale
● High protein supports lean muscle gain.
● Fiber promotes digestion and satiety.
● Controlled carbs prevent insulin spikes for PCOS.
● North Indian meals ensure cultural acceptability and adherence.
Alternatives / Substitutions
● Paneer ↔ Tofu / Cottage Cheese
● Brown Rice ↔ Quinoa / Millet
● Almonds ↔ Walnuts or Cashews
● Milk ↔ Soy Milk / Almond Milk
