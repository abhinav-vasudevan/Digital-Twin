Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals.
2. Avoid fried foods and refined sugar.
3. Eat smaller portions using smaller plates.
4. Walk 20–30 minutes after meals or perform moderate exercise.
5. Include high protein and fiber in every meal.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours per day.
7. Cook with minimal oil.
8. Use low GI foods to control insulin.
9. Include strength training or yoga to manage stress and hormones.
10. Follow consistently for 12 weeks.
Rationale
● High fiber + protein enhances satiety and supports fat loss.
● Low GI foods regulate insulin, important for PCOS management.

● Moderate activity allows slightly higher calorie intake while still promoting weight loss.
● South Indian meals ensure adherence and cultural compatibility.
