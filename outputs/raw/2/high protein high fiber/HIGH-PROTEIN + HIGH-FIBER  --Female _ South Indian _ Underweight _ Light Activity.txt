Tips for Healthy Weight Gain + High Protein & Fiber
1. Eat every 3–4 hours (5–6 meals/day)
2. Include protein in every meal (dal, egg, paneer, tofu)
3. Use healthy oils (coconut, groundnut, sesame)

4. Include legumes, millets, vegetables, and fruits daily
5. Light walking or stretching post meals
6. Sleep 7–8 hours nightly
7. Track portion sizes using katori/bowl method
8. Consistency for 8–12 weeks
Rationale & Alternatives
● Focuses on protein-rich legumes, tofu, nuts, seeds, and whole grains, improving
digestion and healthy weight gain
● Alternatives:
○ Ragi ↔ Millet
○ Moong Dal ↔ Masoor Dal
○ Almond Milk ↔ Soy Milk
○ Makhana ↔ Peanuts
