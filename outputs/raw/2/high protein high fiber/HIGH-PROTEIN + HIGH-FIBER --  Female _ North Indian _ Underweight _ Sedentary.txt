Tips for Weight Gain + PCOS
1. Eat every 2–3 hours with nutrient-dense meals.
2. Include high-protein and healthy fats in every meal.
3. Use moderate GI carbs for steady energy.
4. Include strength exercises 2–3 times a week.
5. Drink water, but not excessively before meals.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
7. Avoid refined sugar and fried foods.
8. Be consistent for 12 weeks.
Rationale
● High protein + fiber supports lean muscle gain and regulates hormones.
● Moderate GI carbs help prevent insulin spikes, important for PCOS.
● Nutrient-dense foods help healthy weight gain without fat accumulation.
● North Indian meals ensure cultural compatibility and adherence.
