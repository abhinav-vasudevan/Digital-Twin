Tips for Heavy Activity + High Protein/Fiber Diet
1. Ensure protein at every meal for muscle recovery.
2. Include complex carbs for sustained energy.
3. Eat fiber-rich foods for digestion and fullness.
4. Hydrate 3–3.5 L/day.
5. Include strength training or moderate cardio to complement diet.
6. Avoid fried and sugary foods.
7. Sleep 7–8 hours for recovery.
Rationale
● High protein supports muscle repair and satiety.
● Fiber regulates digestion and blood sugar.
● Complex carbs fuel heavy activity.
● Healthy fats support energy and hormonal health.

● South Indian meals preserve cultural preference while providing high nutrition.
