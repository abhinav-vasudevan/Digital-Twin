Tips for Moderate Activity + High Protein/Fiber Diet
1. Include protein at every meal (dal, paneer, milk, yogurt).
2. Fiber-rich foods aid digestion and satiety.
3. Avoid fried and sugary foods.
4. Stay hydrated (2.5–3 L/day).
5. Light daily activity like brisk walking helps maintain metabolism.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours for optimal health.
Rationale
● Protein supports lean muscle and satiety.
● Fiber improves digestion and blood sugar regulation.
● Balanced carbs provide energy for moderate activity.
● Healthy fats support brain and hormone function.
● South Indian meals adapted for local taste and adherence.
