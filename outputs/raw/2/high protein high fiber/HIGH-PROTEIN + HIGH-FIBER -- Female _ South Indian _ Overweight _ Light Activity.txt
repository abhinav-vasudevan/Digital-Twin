Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals.
2. Avoid fried foods and sugary drinks.
3. Eat smaller portions using smaller plates.
4. Take a 15–20 min walk after meals.
5. Include fiber and protein in each meal.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
7. Cook with minimal oil.
8. Use low GI foods to prevent insulin spikes.
9. Manage stress with yoga or meditation.
10. Follow consistently for 12 weeks.
Rationale
● Low GI + high fiber helps control blood sugar.
● High protein promotes satiety and lean muscle retention.

● South Indian meals adapted for cultural adherence.
● Portion control and hydration aid in weight loss.
