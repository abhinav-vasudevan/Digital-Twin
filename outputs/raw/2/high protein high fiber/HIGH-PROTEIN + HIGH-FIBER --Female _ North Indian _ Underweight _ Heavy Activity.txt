Tips for Weight Gain + PCOS

1. Eat 5–6 nutrient-dense meals/day.
2. Include protein + healthy fats in every meal.
3. Moderate GI carbs for sustained energy.
4. Include heavy activity or strength exercises 4–5 times/week.
5. Avoid fried & ultra-processed foods.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours/day.
7. Be consistent for at least 12 weeks.
Rationale
● High protein + fiber supports lean mass gain and stabilizes blood sugar.
● Moderate GI carbs provide sustained energy for heavy activity.
● Nutrient-dense foods support hormone balance in PCOS.
● North Indian meals improve adherence and cultural suitability.
