Tips for Healthy Weight Gain + High Protein & Fiber
1. Drink water 20 min before meals
2. Include nuts, seeds, and legumes daily
3. Avoid fried and processed foods
4. Eat consistently 5–6 times/day
5. Include protein in every meal
6. Sleep 7–8 hours
7. Light stretching or yoga daily

8. Track portion sizes using katori/bowl method
9. Be consistent for 8–12 weeks
Rationale & Alternatives
● Focuses on protein-rich legumes, tofu, nuts, seeds, and whole grains, improving
digestion and healthy weight gain
● Alternatives:
○ Ragi ↔ Millet
○ Moong Dal ↔ Masoor Dal
○ Almond Milk ↔ Soy Milk
○ Makhana ↔ Peanuts
