TIPS FOR WEIGHT LOSS (LIGHT
ACTIVITY – FEMALE)
1. Drink 2.5–3 liters water per day.
2. Walk 5000–7000 steps daily (minimum).
3. Avoid tea + biscuits, namkeen, bhujia, chips, bakery foods.
4. Use only ½–1 tsp oil in sabzi preparations.
5. Prefer steamed / sautéed foods instead of fried foods.
6. Stop eating after 8 PM.
7. Sleep 7–8 hours every night to control hunger hormones.
8. Take protein in every meal: dal + chilla + sprouts + paneer + chana.

9. Limit sugar completely (only natural sugars from fruit).
10. Use small plates to control portion size.
11. Avoid drinking water during meals; drink 20 minutes before or after.
12. Include fiber daily: salad, fruits, dal, vegetables, dalia, oats.
13. Limit roti to 2 per meal (never 3).
14. Prefer brown rice, multigrain roti, dalia, moong dal.
15. Limit outside food to once per week.
RATIONALE (WHY THIS DIET WORKS)
● High Protein:
Increases satiety, reduces cravings, supports muscle tone, boosts metabolism.
● High Fiber:
Improves digestion, reduces hunger, stabilizes blood sugar, aids fat loss.
● Low Oil + Low Calorie Meals:
Ensures healthy calorie deficit for weight loss without weakness.
● Light Activity Profile:
Since daily movement is low, the diet provides controlled calories (1300–1400 kcal).
● Balanced North Indian Meals:
Easy to follow long-term because foods are familiar and culturally suitable.
● Even Distribution of Carbs + Protein:
Keeps energy stable and prevents overeating in evenings.
● 3–4 Small Meals:
Prevents long gaps, controls acidity, keeps metabolism active.
