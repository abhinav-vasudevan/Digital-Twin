Tips for Weight Loss + High Protein/Fiber Diet
1. Drink water 20 min before meals
2. Avoid fried and processed foods
3. Use smaller plates to control portions
4. Walk 10–15 min after meals
5. Include more raw vegetables in meals
6. Sleep 7–8 hours nightly
7. Avoid sugary drinks
8. Cook with minimal oil (½ tsp per dish)
9. Incorporate light exercise/yoga
10. Be consistent for 8–12 weeks
Rationale
● High protein promotes satiety and muscle maintenance
● High fiber improves digestion and stabilizes blood sugar
● Low-GI carbohydrate sources prevent insulin spikes

● Balanced meals help with sustainable weight loss
Alternatives
● Brown Rice ↔ Quinoa, Foxtail Millet
● Moong Dal ↔ Masoor Dal, Chana Dal
● Paneer ↔ Tofu
● Almonds ↔ Walnuts or Cashews (moderate quantity)
