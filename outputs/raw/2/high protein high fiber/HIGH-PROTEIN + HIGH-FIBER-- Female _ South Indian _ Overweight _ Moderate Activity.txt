Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals.
2. Avoid fried and processed foods.
3. Eat smaller portions using smaller plates.
4. Take a 15–20 min walk after meals.
5. Include protein and fiber in every meal.
6. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
7. Cook with minimal oil.
8. Include low GI foods to regulate insulin.
9. Practice yoga or light exercises to manage stress.
10. Follow consistently for 12 weeks.

Rationale
● High fiber + protein helps control blood sugar, improves satiety, and promotes fat loss.
● Low GI carbohydrates prevent insulin spikes, crucial for PCOS management.
● South Indian meals ensure cultural adherence and meal satisfaction.
