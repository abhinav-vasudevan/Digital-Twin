Tips for High-Protein + High-Fiber Diet
1. Include protein and fiber in all meals and snacks.
2. Avoid refined carbs, fried foods, and sugary drinks.
3. Light daily activity (walking, yoga) 20–30 min.
4. Drink 2.5–3 L water/day.
5. Sleep 7–8 hours/day.
6. Maintain consistent meal timings.
Rationale
● Protein supports lean muscle, satiety, and energy.
● Fiber improves digestion and stabilizes blood sugar.
● Balanced carbs prevent spikes in energy and hunger.
● North Indian meals increase adherence and taste preference.
Alternatives / Substitutions
● Paneer ↔ Tofu
● Brown Rice ↔ Quinoa / Millet
● Almonds ↔ Walnuts / Cashews
● Milk ↔ Soy Milk / Almond Milk
● Seasonal vegetables and fruits
