MEAL SCHEDULE & OPTIONS

seeds ½ tsp
Method: Mix and drink warm
Serving Size: 1 glass

Prep Time: 5 min
Nutritive Values: 40 kcal | 1 g protein | 7 g carbs | 1 g fat | 3 g fiber

Option 2: Vegetable Oats Upma + Boiled Egg

Nutritive Values: 300 kcal | 18 g protein | 40 g carbs | 8 g fat | 7 g fiber

RATIONALE & ALTERNATIVES
This plan provides high-quality protein and fiber to maintain healthy weight, support metabolism,
improve digestion, and stabilize energy. Suitable for sedentary lifestyle requiring controlled
calories.
Alternatives:
Chicken ↔ Fish ↔ Eggs
Brown rice ↔ Millets ↔ Quinoa
