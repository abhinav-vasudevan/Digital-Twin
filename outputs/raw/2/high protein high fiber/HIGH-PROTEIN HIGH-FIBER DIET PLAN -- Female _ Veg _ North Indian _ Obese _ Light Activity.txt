Dietary & Cultural Context
Diet Type: Vegetarian
Cuisine Style: North Indian
Cooking Oil: Mustard oil / Groundnut oil
Preferred Flavors: Mild spice, low salt
Allergens: Gluten, dairy, nuts
Tips for Weight Loss (High Protein + High
Fiber)
1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals
2. Add salad before lunch & dinner
3. Avoid wheat at night

4. Include protein in every meal
5. Avoid juices, sweets, bakery
6. Walk 15 minutes after meals
7. Sleep 7–8 hours
8. No fried snacks
9. Use minimal oil
10. Maintain consistency for 8–12 weeks
Rationale & Alternatives
• High protein improves metabolism
• High fiber improves digestion, gut health, satiety
• Balanced meals prevent cravings
Alternatives:
