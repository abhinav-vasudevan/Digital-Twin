Tips for Weight Loss & High Fiber Diet

1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals.
2. Avoid sugar, juices, bakery items.
3. Walk 10 minutes after meals.
4. Stop eating after 8 PM.
5. Increase protein in all meals.
6. Add a salad before lunch & dinner.
7. Reduce wheat; increase millet/quinoa.
8. Sleep 7–8 hours.
9. Avoid fried foods completely.
10. Maintain routine for at least 3 months.
Rationale & Alternatives
• High-protein meals maintain metabolism during weight loss.
• High-fiber foods support satiety & digestion.
• Complex carbs prevent sugar spikes.
