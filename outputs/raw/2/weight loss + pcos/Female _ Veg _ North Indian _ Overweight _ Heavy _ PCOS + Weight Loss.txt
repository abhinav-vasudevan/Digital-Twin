Rationale & Alternatives
You perform high-intensity cardio, so you need:
• Slow-release carbohydrates (millets, oats, quinoa)
• High plant protein (moong dal, paneer, chana, sprouts)
• Anti-inflammatory foods for PCOS (flaxseed, cinnamon, turmeric, chia)
• Low GI foods to control insulin spikes

Alternatives:
Paneer ↔ Tofu
Wheat Roti ↔ Ragi Roti
Brown Rice ↔ Little Millet
Regular Poha ↔ Red Poha
