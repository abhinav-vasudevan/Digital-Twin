TIPS FOR WEIGHT LOSS + PCOS
1. Eat dinner before 8 PM.
2. Avoid wheat daily—rotate with ragi, jowar, millets.
3. No sugar, jaggery, packaged juices.
4. Include 15 minutes walking after meals.
5. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.
6. Avoid deep-fried foods completely.
7. Include protein with every meal.
8. Do not skip meals; maintain timing.
9. Reduce milk; prefer curd or buttermilk.
10. Manage stress (PCOS worsens with stress).
RATIONALE & ALTERNATIVES
This diet emphasises:
• Low-GI millets → stabilise insulin
• High protein → fat loss + PCOS hormone balance
• Anti-inflammatory foods → reduce androgen symptoms
• Lower carbs in evening → improved fat burn
Alternatives:
Ragi ↔ Jowar
Foxtail Millet ↔ Little Millet
Moong Dal ↔ Masoor Dal

Coconut Chutney ↔ Mint Chutney
