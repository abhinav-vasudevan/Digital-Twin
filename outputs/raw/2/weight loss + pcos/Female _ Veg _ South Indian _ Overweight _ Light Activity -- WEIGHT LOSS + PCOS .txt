Rationale & Alternatives
• Low-GI foods help control insulin resistance in PCOS
• High protein supports fat loss and reduces cravings
• Millet ↔ Brown rice
• Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
• Ragi ↔ Oats

• Curd ↔ Buttermilk
• Paneer ↔ Tofu
