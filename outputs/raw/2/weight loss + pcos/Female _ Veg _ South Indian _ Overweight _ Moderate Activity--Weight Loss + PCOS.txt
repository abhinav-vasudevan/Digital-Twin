Rationale & Alternatives
• Low-GI foods stabilize insulin (key for PCOS)
• High fiber improves hormones & reduces cravings
• Healthy fats (flaxseed, peanuts) support ovulation
• Alternatives:
– Paneer ↔ Tofu
– Brown rice ↔ Millets
– Curd ↔ Unsweetened soy curd
– Sprouts ↔ Soaked legumes
