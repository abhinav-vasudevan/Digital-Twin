Measurements
1. Glass Measurements (Indian kitchen)
• Small glass = 150 ml
• Medium glass = 200 ml
• Large glass = 250–300 ml

2. Bowl (Katori) Measurements
• Small bowl = 80–100 ml (about 80–90 g for cooked food)
• Medium bowl = 150–180 ml (about 150–170 g cooked food)
• Large bowl = 250–300 ml (about 250–270 g cooked food)
3. Spoon Measurements
• 1 teaspoon (tsp) = 5 ml (about 4–5 g)
• ½ teaspoon = 2.5 ml
• ¼ teaspoon = 1.25 ml
• 1 tablespoon (tbsp) = 15 ml (about 12–15 g)
• ½ tablespoon = 7.5 ml
4. Cup Measurements
• 1 standard measuring cup = 240 ml
• ½ cup = 120 ml
