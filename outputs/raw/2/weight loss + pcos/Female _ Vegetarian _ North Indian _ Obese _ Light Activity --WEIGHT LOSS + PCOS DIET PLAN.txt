Rationale & Alternatives
Low-GI, high-fiber foods help control insulin spikes in PCOS.
Protein increases satiety and supports weight loss.
Healthy fats reduce inflammation.
Millets, quinoa, dal, and vegetables improve hormone balance.
Alternatives:
Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
Brown rice ↔ Quinoa ↔ Millets
Paneer ↔ Tofu
Potato ↔ Sweet potato
