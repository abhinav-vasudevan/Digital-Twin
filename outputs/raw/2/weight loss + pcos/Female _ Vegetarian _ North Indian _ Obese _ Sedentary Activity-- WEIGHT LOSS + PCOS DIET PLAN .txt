Rationale & Alternatives
• Low-GI foods keep insulin stable
• Protein supports satiety and fat loss
• Fiber improves PCOS symptoms and digestion
• Anti-inflammatory foods reduce cyst-related inflammation
Alternatives:
Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
Brown rice ↔ Quinoa ↔ Millets
Potato ↔ Sweet potato (better GI)
Paneer ↔ Tofu
