Allergens: Coconut, nuts (adjust as needed)
Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Avoid all refined sugar
2. No fruit juices
3. Prefer low-GI foods (millets, quinoa, legumes)
4. Avoid dairy completely
5. Include seeds daily (flaxseed, chia, pumpkin)
6. Walk 10 minutes after every meal
7. Sleep 7–8 hours
8. Reduce stress (yoga/breathing)
9. Prefer steamed/boiled foods
10. Maintain consistency 8–12 weeks
Rationale & Alternatives
• Low GI foods help reduce insulin resistance
• High-fiber meals improve satiety and PCOS symptoms
• Plant-based proteins aid weight loss
Alternatives:
• Millet ↔ Brown rice
• Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
• Coconut milk ↔ Almond milk
• Quinoa ↔ Broken wheat
