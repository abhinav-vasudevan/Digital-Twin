Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Drink water 20 minutes before meals
2. Avoid fried and processed foods
3. Use smaller plates
4. Take a 10–15 min walk after meals
5. Replace sweets with fruits or nuts
6. Sleep 7–8 hours

7. Avoid fruit juices
8. Cook with minimal oil
9. Light yoga or stretching daily
10. Be consistent for 12 weeks
Rationale & Alternatives
● Supports fat loss, hormone balance, reduces insulin spikes, and improves digestion
through low-GI foods, high fiber, and vegan protein.
● Alternatives:
○ Quinoa ↔ Brown Rice
○ Moong Dal ↔ Masoor Dal
○ Almond Milk ↔ Soy Milk
○ Makhana ↔ Peanuts
