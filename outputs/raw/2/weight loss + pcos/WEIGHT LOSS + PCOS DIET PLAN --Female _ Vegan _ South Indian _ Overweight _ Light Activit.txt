Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Walk 10–15 minutes after meals
2. Avoid sugar, juice, bakery items
3. Use small plates
4. Choose low-GI millets
5. Take 35 g fiber daily

6. Avoid dairy (vegan)
7. Sleep 7–8 hours
8. Reduce stress
9. Avoid fruit at night
10. Be consistent 12+ weeks
Rationale & Alternatives
This plan lowers insulin spikes and inflammation while increasing plant protein, improving PCOS
and fat loss.
