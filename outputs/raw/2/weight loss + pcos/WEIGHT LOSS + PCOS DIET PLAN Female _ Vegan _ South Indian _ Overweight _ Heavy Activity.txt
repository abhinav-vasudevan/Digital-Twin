1. Prefer low-GI millets
2. Avoid dairy fully
3. Walk 10 minutes after meals
4. Cut sugar completely
5. Sleep 7–8 hours
6. High-fiber meals daily
7. Include seeds daily
8. Avoid fruit juices
9. Eat early dinner
10. Maintain consistency
