Rationale & Alternatives
This plan creates a calorie deficit while ensuring enough protein for fat loss and muscle
preservation.
Low-fat non-veg choices support metabolism without heavy digestion load.
Alternatives:
• Chicken ↔ Fish ↔ Eggs
• Brown Rice ↔ Millet ↔ Red Rice
• Chapati ↔ Ragi Roti
