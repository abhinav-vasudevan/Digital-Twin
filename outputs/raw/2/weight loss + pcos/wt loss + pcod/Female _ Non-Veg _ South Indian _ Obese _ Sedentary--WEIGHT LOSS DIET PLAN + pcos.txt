Profile: Female | Non-Veg | South Indian | Obese | Sedentary

Dietary & Cultural Context
Diet Type: Non-Vegetarian
Cuisine Style: South Indian
Cooking Oil: Cold-pressed groundnut or coconut oil
Allergens: Eggs, fish, nuts (adjust if allergic)

Rationale & Alternatives
This diet lowers calories while providing enough protein to prevent muscle loss.
Low-oil, high-fiber South Indian foods help digestion and fat burning.
Alternatives:
• Chicken ↔ Fish ↔ Eggs
• Brown rice ↔ Millet ↔ Red rice
• Wheat chapati ↔ Ragi chapati
