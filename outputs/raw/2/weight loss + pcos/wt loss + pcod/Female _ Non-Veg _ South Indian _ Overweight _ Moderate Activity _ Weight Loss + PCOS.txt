Dietary & Cultural Context
Diet Type: Non-Vegetarian
Cuisine: South Indian
Allergens: Dairy, eggs, gluten (optional), fish
Cooking Style: Minimal oil, low GI foods for PCOS
No deep fried foods
Avoid sugar completely
Rationale & Alternatives
Low GI meals keep insulin stable (important in PCOS)
High protein improves metabolism and fat-burn
Anti-inflammatory foods reduce bloating and cravings
