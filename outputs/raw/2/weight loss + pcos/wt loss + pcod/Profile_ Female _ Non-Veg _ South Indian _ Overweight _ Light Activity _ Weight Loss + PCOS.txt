Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Avoid white rice, sweets, and bakery foods.
2. Include protein with every meal.
3. Walk 10 minutes after lunch/dinner.
4. Avoid long gaps between meals.
5. No fruit juices—only whole fruits.
6. Take consistent sleep (7–8 hours).
7. Reduce gluten-heavy meals at night.
8. Have 2.5–3 L water daily.
9. Include one anti-inflammatory food daily: turmeric, cinnamon, ginger.
10. Avoid deep-fried foods completely.
Rationale & Alternatives

This PCOS-friendly plan stabilizes insulin and reduces inflammation using:
• Low GI carbs
• High protein meals
• Healthy fats
• Omega-3 from fish
• Minimal oil, high fiber
