Tips for PCOS Weight Loss
1. Eat every 3–4 hours to maintain insulin balance
2. Choose low-GI grains (millet, quinoa, oats)
3. Avoid sugar, jaggery, honey
4. Avoid deep-fried foods

5. Include 25–30 min walk daily
6. Stop screen-time while eating
7. Sleep 7–8 hours daily
8. Add 1 cup vegetables per meal
9. Drink 3 L water daily
10. Avoid dairy & gluten (improves PCOS symptoms)
Rationale & Alternatives
• Low GI meals → stabilize insulin (core of PCOS)
• High fiber → reduces cravings
• Vegan protein sources → tofu, dal, quinoa
• Healthy fats → nuts, seeds → hormone balance
Alternatives:
✔ Quinoa ↔ Brown rice
✔ Tofu ↔ Moong dal
✔ Millet ↔ Oats
✔ Coconut chutney ↔ Mint chutney
