Rationale & Alternatives
Low-GI foods stabilize insulin (most important for PCOS)
High fiber reduces inflammation
Plant proteins support weight loss without deficiency
Evening meals kept light to reduce insulin spikes
Alternatives:
Millet ↔ Brown rice
Moong dal ↔ Masoor dal
Soy milk ↔ Almond milk
Peanut chutney ↔ Sesame chutney
