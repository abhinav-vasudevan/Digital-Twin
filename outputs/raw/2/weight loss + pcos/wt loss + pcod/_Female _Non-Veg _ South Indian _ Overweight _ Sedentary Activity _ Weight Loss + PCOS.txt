Tips for Weight Loss + PCOS
1. Avoid rice at night.
2. No sugar, fruit juices, bakery items.
3. Include protein at every meal.
4. Walk 10 minutes after meals.
5. Sleep 7–8 hours daily.

6. Avoid long gaps between meals.
7. Prefer low-GI foods: millets, oats, brown rice.
8. Increase fiber: vegetables, chia, flax.
9. Have 2.5–3 L water daily.
10. Reduce high-estrogen foods like soy if PCOS symptoms worsen.
Rationale & Alternatives
This plan supports weight loss + PCOS management through:
• Low-GI carbohydrates
• High-protein meals
• Anti-inflammatory foods
• Low oil, home-style cooking
• Balanced macronutrients for insulin stability
