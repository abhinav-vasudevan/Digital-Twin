Substitutions & Rationale (Shortcut)
Grains: Ragi / Millet → Quinoa, Barley, Oats
Proteins: Tofu → Tempeh / Edamame; Sprouts → Chickpeas / Kidney beans
Dairy: Almond milk / Curd → Soy milk / Unsweetened coconut yogurt
Oils: Gingelly / Groundnut → Olive / Avocado oil
Snacks: Makhana → Pumpkin / Sunflower seeds; Herbal tea → Green / Cinnamon tea
Fruits: Apple → Pear / Guava / Berries

Rationale:
● Low GI foods for blood sugar control
● High fiber & controlled calories for weight loss
● Plant proteins preserve lean muscle
● Healthy fats for metabolic health
● Anti-inflammatory herbs and teas
● Steam, boil, sauté; avoid deep-fry
● Early hydration and soups curb cravings
Dietary Guidelines
1. Prefer low GI foods – ragi, millets, oats, brown rice.
2. Avoid white rice, sugar, and refined flour.
3. Include fiber-rich vegetables (bottle gourd, beans, spinach).
4. Drink 2.5–3 L water daily.
5. Limit oil to 2 tsp/day (use gingelly or groundnut oil).
6. Choose steamed or sautéed cooking methods, avoid deep fry.
7. Walk or stretch 45 min/day to enhance insulin sensitivity.
